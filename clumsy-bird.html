Clumsy Bird

A MelonJS made "Flappy Bird" clone.

Play online at http://ellisonleao.github.io/clumsy-bird/

Running Locally

  • Install Node and Grunt
  • Install the dependencies
npm install

Then just type on your shell:

grunt connect

Open your browser at http://localhost:8001/
