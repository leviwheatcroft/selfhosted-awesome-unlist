Elkarbackup

What is it?

ElkarBackup is a free open-source backup solution based on RSync/RSnapshot

Features

  • Centralized backups
  • Easy-to-use web interface
  • Backup Linux/Windows clients
  • Instant recovery!
  • Pre-scripts & Post-scripts
  • Multilanguage (English, Basque, Spanish, German) - Help us to translate Elkarbackup in your language!
